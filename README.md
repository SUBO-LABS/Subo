## What is Subo?
Subo isn't just another chatbot. It's a **multimodal AI operating system** that lives entirely inside your browser. Instead of switching between tabs and tools, Subo brings advanced AI capabilities directly to the websites you already use.

## Core Capabilities

### Total Browser Automation
Why click when you can type? Tell Subo to *"Book my flight to London,"* or *"Scrape these leads,"* and watch it navigate, click, fill forms, and type across multiple tabs completely autonomously.

### Connect to Anything
Subo lives on any website. It seamlessly interfaces with your apps, databases, and internal tools without requiring complex API integrations or OAuth setup.

### Advanced AI Chat
A deeply context-aware assistant that remembers your workflows, understands complex codebases, and answers questions based on the exact page you're currently viewing.

### Multimodal Creation Suite
Go beyond text. Generate stunning images, compose music, write production-ready code, and create entire documents right inside your browser sidebar.
